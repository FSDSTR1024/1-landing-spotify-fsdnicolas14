How does Spotify Premium trial work?

If you've never had Premium before, you may be able to try it for free (or at a reduced price). Even if it's a test, you'll need to specify a valid payment method in order to sign up. We'll use it to confirm the country or region where you live and to charge you for your subscription if you ultimately want to remain Premium after the offer ends. We'll send you a reminder 7 days before the end of the trial. Restrictions may apply depending on the country. Check the conditions.

How do i cancel my Premium plan?

You can easily cancel the Premium plan whenever you want online on your account page.If you cancel your paid Premium membership, you can use it until your next billing date. After that, your account will move to our free service. If you cancel your subscription during a zero-price free trial period, you will lose access to Premium benefits immediately and your account will be switched to our free service. Free trials at no price cannot be reactivated. You will keep all your lists and saved music and you will be able to listen to them with ads on our free service.

How does the Premium Duo plan work?

Premium Duo is a plan for couples who live together. It is cheaper than having two Premium Individual accounts. We'll ask for your address to make sure you live in the same place. You'll be able to invite someone to the plan right after purchase. Each member will have a separate Premium account, so you can listen to content at the same time. Everyone will have their own lists and the music they have saved. The other plan member won't be able to see what you're hearing. Learn more about Premium Duo.

How does the Premium Family Plan work?

Premium Family is a plan for a maximum of 6 family members living together. It is more affordable than having an individual Premium account. We'll ask for your address to make sure you live in the same place. You'll be able to invite other members to the plan right after purchase. Each member will have a separate Premium account, so you can listen to content at the same time. Everyone will have their own lists and the music they have saved. The rest of the plan members won't be able to see what you're hearing. Find out more about Premium Familiar.

How does the Premium Student Plan work?

If you have enrolled in an accredited university or college and are over the age of 18, yes. You can enjoy Premium for Students for a maximum of 4 years. Find out more about Premium for Students.

How much does Spotify Premium cost in Spain?

Spotify Premium prices in Spain vary depending on the Premium plan you choose: Spotify Premium Individual is worth €10.99 per month; Premium Duo, €14.99 per month; Family Premium, €17.99 per month, and Premium for Students, €5.99 per month.
